Welcome to the Grand Slam Tennis Club

Whatever your playing standard, you'll find a game, whether its playing socially or playing in the club competitions, competing in the National leagues. If you are a beginner or are returning to the game and a bit rusty, our tennis and badminton coaches provide a range of coaching activities, both individual & group.


There are one-day fun tournaments in the spring and summer for players who enjoy less serious tennis. These are usually followed by social events such as a barbeque or supper which are open to all members and their families. The club is a member of the Lawn Tennis Association this has many benefits for our members. This includes access to a limited number of Wimbledon tickets every year
